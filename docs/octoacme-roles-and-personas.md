# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Collaborates with **QA/Testing Specialists** on acceptance criteria and test coverage
- Works with **Tech Leads** on technical design and code review feedback
- Receives requirements from **Product Managers** and updates from **Project Managers**

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Partners with **Project Managers** on delivery timelines and dependencies
- Works with **Stakeholders/Sponsors** on prioritization and business alignment
- Collaborates with **Developers** and **Tech Leads** on feasibility and technical trade-offs
- Reviews outcomes with **Support/Operations** teams on customer impact

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Syncs with **Product Managers** on roadmap and backlog prioritization
- Escalates blockers and dependencies with **Tech Leads** and **Developers**
- Reports progress to **Stakeholders/Sponsors** and manages approvals
- Coordinates with **Support/Operations** on release readiness

---

## QA/Testing Specialist

### Role Summary
QA/Testing specialists ensure product quality through comprehensive test planning, execution, and validation. They collaborate with developers and product teams to define acceptance criteria and validate solutions before release.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain test plans aligned with acceptance criteria
- Execute manual and automated test scenarios
- Identify, document, and track defects
- Validate fixes and perform regression testing
- Contribute to Definition of Done and quality standards
- Participate in release readiness reviews
- Provide feedback on testability during design and development phases

### Goals
- Deliver high-quality products with minimal production defects
- Enable fast, confident releases through comprehensive testing
- Reduce rework and incident response costs
- Build shared quality ownership across the team

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning and backlog refinement
- Defect reports and test status updates
- Release readiness sign-off
- Design and code review participation

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Works closely with **Developers** on acceptance criteria and test coverage
- Collaborates with **Product Managers** on feature validation and quality metrics
- Partners with **Tech Leads** on test strategy and automation frameworks
- Coordinates with **Support/Operations** on production issues and regression testing

---

## Tech Lead / Architect

### Role Summary
Tech Leads provide technical guidance and make architectural decisions to ensure solutions are scalable, maintainable, and aligned with technical strategy. They mentor the team and remove technical barriers to delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Guide technical design and architecture decisions
- Review technical proposals and design documents
- Mentor developers and promote technical excellence
- Identify technical risks and propose mitigations
- Ensure alignment with platform standards and practices
- Contribute to technology roadmap and dependency management
- Support estimation and identify technical blockers early

### Goals
- Deliver scalable, maintainable solutions
- Reduce technical debt and architectural risks
- Build team capability and technical standards
- Enable sustainable, predictable delivery

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and architecture discussions
- Code reviews and technical mentoring
- Risk escalations and technical roadmap updates
- Participation in planning and retrospectives

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Guides **Developers** through technical decisions and code reviews
- Partners with **Project Managers** on risk identification and mitigation
- Collaborates with **QA/Testing Specialists** on test strategy and automation
- Advises **Product Managers** on technical trade-offs and feasibility

---

## Stakeholder / Sponsor

### Role Summary
Stakeholders and Sponsors provide business context, prioritization, and approval authority. They represent customer needs, business goals, and organizational strategy. They ensure projects deliver measurable value.

### Responsibilities
- Define business requirements and success criteria
- Provide approval and prioritization decisions
- Communicate project value to leadership and customers
- Escalate risks and resource constraints
- Review and validate outcomes against success metrics
- Provide feedback and strategic guidance
- Champion the project across the organization

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver measurable business value
- Maintain alignment between engineering and business goals
- Reduce rework due to unclear requirements
- Enable efficient resource allocation

### Typical Communication
- Project initiation and approval gates
- Monthly stakeholder updates
- Milestone reviews and release announcements
- Risk escalation and decision-making forums

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Works with **Product Managers** on business requirements and prioritization
- Reviews progress with **Project Managers** and escalates blockers
- Validates outcomes and measures success with **Support/Operations**
- Provides strategic input to **Tech Leads** on technical investments

---

## Scrum Master / Agile Coach

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, remove team blockers, and coach teams on process improvement and agile practices. They foster a culture of continuous improvement and psychological safety.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, standups, reviews, and retrospectives
- Help remove impediments and blockers
- Coach team on agile practices and continuous improvement
- Track and communicate sprint metrics
- Facilitate conflict resolution
- Support Definition of Done and process adherence
- Identify and escalate process bottlenecks

### Goals
- Maximize team velocity and predictability
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement
- Remove friction from the development process
- Build team cohesion and psychological safety

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- Impediment escalation
- Retrospective action item tracking
- Metrics and health checks

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Coaches all team members on agile practices
- Partners with **Project Managers** on planning and risk management
- Facilitates communication between **Developers**, **QA**, and **Tech Leads**
- Escalates blockers to **Product Managers** and **Stakeholders** when needed

---

## Support / Operations

### Role Summary
Support and Operations teams ensure production systems run smoothly and respond to customer issues. They bridge engineering and customers, providing critical feedback that informs product improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Monitor production systems and respond to incidents
- Triage and escalate customer issues
- Validate releases in production
- Provide customer feedback to product and engineering teams
- Maintain runbooks and incident response procedures
- Participate in post-incident retrospectives
- Communicate status and reliability metrics to stakeholders

### Goals
- Maximize system reliability and uptime
- Reduce time to resolution for customer issues
- Provide customer insights that inform product improvements
- Build operational excellence and incident response capability

### Typical Communication
- Release notifications and deployment coordination
- Incident response and escalation
- Customer feedback and support trends
- Post-incident retrospectives

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Reports production issues to **Developers** and **Tech Leads** for resolution
- Coordinates with **Project Managers** on release readiness and rollback plans
- Provides customer feedback to **Product Managers** for prioritization
- Participates in **Scrum Master** ceremonies and retrospectives
