# OctoAcme Onboarding Guide for New Team Members

## Purpose
Help new team members quickly understand OctoAcme's project management approach, roles, and processes.

## First Day Checklist
- [ ] Access to repository and project management tools
- [ ] Slack/communication channels joined
- [ ] Calendar access for recurring meetings
- [ ] Introduction to team members (especially your direct lead/mentor)
- [ ] Overview of current projects and roadmap

## First Week Checklist
- [ ] Read the [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Read the [Roles and Personas guide](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md)
- [ ] Attend sprint planning or standup to see processes in action
- [ ] Meet with your role lead or mentor for 1:1 orientation
- [ ] Review the [Cross-Functional Collaboration Guide](cross-functional-collaboration-guide.md)
- [ ] Access the current project charter or one-pager

## First Month Checklist
- [ ] Complete orientation for your specific role (see role-specific sections below)
- [ ] Participate in your first retrospective
- [ ] Understand the decision-making process for your role
- [ ] Identify a mentor or peer for ongoing questions
- [ ] Shadow or pair with an experienced team member on key activities

## Role-Specific Onboarding

### Developers
**Key Documents**: [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md), [Project Planning](octoacme-project-planning.md)

**First Week Focus**:
- [ ] Repository setup (clone, branches, local environment)
- [ ] Review current sprint backlog and acceptance criteria
- [ ] Understand Definition of Done for the team
- [ ] Pair with Tech Lead on codebase architecture
- [ ] Understand testing and code review expectations

**Key Questions to Answer**:
- Where is the Definition of Done documented?
- What's the branch naming and PR review process?
- How does the team estimate work (story points, t-shirt sizing)?
- Who do I ask for technical guidance?

**Communication Channels**:
- Daily standup (15 min)
- Slack channel for technical questions
- Code review comments on PRs

---

### Product Managers
**Key Documents**: [Project Initiation](octoacme-project-initiation.md), [Project Planning](octoacme-project-planning.md)

**First Week Focus**:
- [ ] Review current roadmap and product strategy
- [ ] Understand customer segments and use cases
- [ ] Meet with stakeholders and sponsors
- [ ] Review past project charters and success metrics
- [ ] Understand the backlog prioritization process

**Key Questions to Answer**:
- What are the current top priorities and why?
- Who are the key stakeholders and their goals?
- How do we measure product success?
- What's the cadence for roadmap updates?

**Communication Channels**:
- Weekly PM + PdM sync
- Roadmap planning sessions
- Stakeholder update forums

---

### Project Managers
**Key Documents**: [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md), [Risk Management & Communication](octoacme-risks-and-communication.md)

**First Week Focus**:
- [ ] Review current project charter and timeline
- [ ] Understand the team structure and resource allocation
- [ ] Learn the risk register and escalation process
- [ ] Set up status reporting cadence
- [ ] Review past retrospectives and action items

**Key Questions to Answer**:
- What are the current active projects and their status?
- Who are the key stakeholders for each project?
- What's the escalation path and decision-making process?
- How do we track progress and communicate status?

**Communication Channels**:
- Weekly stakeholder updates
- Daily standup facilitation
- Risk review meetings

---

### QA/Testing Specialists
**Key Documents**: [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md), [Project Planning](octoacme-project-planning.md)

**First Week Focus**:
- [ ] Review Definition of Done and quality standards
- [ ] Understand test frameworks and automation tools
- [ ] Meet with Developers and Tech Lead on testability
- [ ] Review current test plans and coverage
- [ ] Understand release readiness criteria

**Key Questions to Answer**:
- What are our quality standards and test coverage targets?
- What's the testing process (manual, automated, both)?
- How do we handle defects and regressions?
- What does "release ready" mean?

**Communication Channels**:
- Sprint planning and backlog refinement
- Defect tracking and reporting
- Release readiness reviews

---

### Tech Leads / Architects
**Key Documents**: [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md), [Project Planning](octoacme-project-planning.md)

**First Week Focus**:
- [ ] Review system architecture and technical strategy
- [ ] Understand current technical debt and risks
- [ ] Meet with development team on coding standards
- [ ] Review technical design process (ADRs, design docs)
- [ ] Understand technology roadmap

**Key Questions to Answer**:
- What's the overall technical architecture?
- What are the top technical risks and debt items?
- What's our process for technical decisions?
- What coding standards and practices are we following?

**Communication Channels**:
- Technical design reviews
- Code review comments
- Architecture discussions in planning

---

### Scrum Masters / Agile Coaches
**Key Documents**: [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md), [Retrospective & Continuous Improvement](octoacme-retrospective-and-continuous-improvement.md)

**First Week Focus**:
- [ ] Review current sprint cadence and ceremonies
- [ ] Understand team composition and dynamics
- [ ] Learn the Definition of Done and process agreements
- [ ] Review past retrospectives and action items
- [ ] Meet with all team members individually

**Key Questions to Answer**:
- What sprint length are we using?
- What's the team's current pain point or focus area?
- How do we facilitate retrospectives?
- What process improvements are in flight?

**Communication Channels**:
- Daily standup facilitation
- Retrospective meetings
- Team health checks

---

### Support / Operations
**Key Documents**: [Release & Deployment](octoacme-release-and-deployment.md), [Risk Management & Communication](octoacme-risks-and-communication.md)

**First Week Focus**:
- [ ] Review runbooks and incident response procedures
- [ ] Understand production environment and monitoring
- [ ] Meet with Developers on common issues
- [ ] Review recent incidents and postmortems
- [ ] Understand release coordination process

**Key Questions to Answer**:
- How do we monitor production systems?
- What's the incident response process?
- How do we coordinate releases with engineering?
- What are the top issues customers are experiencing?

**Communication Channels**:
- Incident response channels
- Release coordination meetings
- Customer feedback channels

---

### Stakeholders / Sponsors
**Key Documents**: [Project Initiation](octoacme-project-initiation.md), [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md)

**First Week Focus**:
- [ ] Understand the project's business case and goals
- [ ] Review success metrics and reporting cadence
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager and Product Manager
- [ ] Learn the approval process for key decisions
- [ ] Understand escalation and risk process

**Key Questions to Answer**:
- What are the strategic goals for this project?
- What does success look like (metrics)?
- What's my role in decision-making and approvals?
- How will progress be communicated to me?

**Communication Channels**:
- Monthly stakeholder updates
- Key milestone reviews
- Escalation and decision forums

- **Questions about your role?** → Talk to your direct lead or role mentor

- **Technical questions?** → Ask your Tech Lead or pair with a developer
- **General questions?** → Slack the team channel or ask in standup
